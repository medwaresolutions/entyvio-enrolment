  • ENTYVIO PATHWAY TERMS OF USE

     
    Entyvio Pathway eligibility criteria

    Entyvio Pathway is available to all patients prescribed ENTYVIO.

    To receive IV infusions in the home or other approved location, the treating doctor considers home infusions to be necessary (e.g. clinical reasons, infusion clinic at capacity, no affiliation to an infusion clinic) and clinically appropriate. 


    Terms and Conditions

    Your doctor has prescribed ENTYVIO (vedolizumab) for you

    Your doctor has explained the program to you, and you understand and consent to participating in the program which may include home infusions or self-injection training provided by a qualified nurse from Ascott Sales Integration Pty Ltd (“Ascott”). 

    You agree to communications via SMS, email and phone from the Ascott program coordinator for confirmation of appointments, satisfaction surveys and to follow-up on any adverse events identified as part of this program, which may include Takeda contacting your healthcare team about adverse events

    You agree to communication between the Ascott nurse and your healthcare team regarding your therapy as well as your private health fund if relevant

    You understand that this program does not oblige you to continue your current treatment, but if your treatment changes you may no longer be eligible for this program

    You understand that you can opt out of the program and/or its services at any time by consulting with your doctor and advising Ascott on 1800 719 663 or via email: support@entyviopathway.com.au 

    You understand that Takeda provides this service to you and reserves the right to discontinue this service at any given time. In this case, Ascott will inform you about this decision and discuss further details and options with you

    You understand that your personal information will be collected, stored, used and disclosed in accordance with Australian Privacy legislation and Takeda’s Privacy Notice. This information will only be shared with relevant parties providing this service in accordance with consent granted for data processing as described in the Privacy Notice.
